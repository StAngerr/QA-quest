A long time ago in a far far away galaxy there was a planet Testoniya inhabited by many robots. They were investigating surrounding planet and looking for new.


Once the robots of Testonia received the signal from the formerly unknown planet. Robot Cobalt received this signal and decided to go on expedition to learn more about the new planet and its inhabitants. But during the flight system error occured and robot lost control in the ship. Autopilot has landed it on the nearest planet (maybe it was even the Earth).


Unfortunately, the landing was not completely successful and some details of the ship were lost. Cobalt faced the task to find the lost parts and continue his expedition...
